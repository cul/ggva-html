All Saints Episcopal Church (Pasadena, Calif.). Rectory, 132 North Euclid Avenue.
Rectory for All saints episcopal church, Pasadena, Cal. : / Greene & Greene, Architects. 1902 7 drawings various media 47.5 x 51.3 cm. (18 3/4 x 20 1/4 in.) or smaller

Job no. 60.
Date and address taken from A Greene & Greene guide, by Janaan Strand, Pasadena, 1974, p. 38.
Scale for drawings is 1/4"=1', 1/2"=1' or 1 1/2"=1'.
This set consists of 4 ink drawings with pencil on cloth and 3 ink drawings with pencil on recto and watercolor wash applied to the verso on cloth.
