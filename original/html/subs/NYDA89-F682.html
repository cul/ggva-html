Hubert F. Krantz House (Palm Beach, Fla.)
Hubert F. Krantz [house, Prospect Park, Palm Beach, Fla.] / Greene & Greene, Arch'ts. 1919 25 sheets various media 89.3 x 132.8 cm.(35 1/2 x 52 3/8 in.) or smaller

Scale varies.
Job no. 392.
"Meanwhile, in 1919, while Charles was engrossed with the James house, Henry was carrying on with the slim practice coming into Pasadena office. His major concerns were yet another scheme for John H. Poole and an elaborate project, a large two-story Spanish Colonial Revival house, for Hubert F. Krantz. Neither was built." -- Greene & Greene, architecture as a fine art, by Randell Makinson, Salt Lake City, 1977, p. 229. The location for the project is also taken from this book, p. 232.
This set consists of 11 blueprints on paper, 9 pencil drawings on tracing paper, 2 brownline prints on tracing paper, 1 ink drawing on cloth, 1 pencil drawing on graph paper, 1 pencil drawing on tracing paper with pencil applied to the verso, and 1 brownline print on paper.
