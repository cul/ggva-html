Henry A. Ware House (Pasadena, Calif.)
Residence for Henry A. Ware Esq. at Pasadena, Cal. / Greene and Greene, Arch'ts. 1913 27 sheets various media 86.3 x 98.5 cm. (34 x 38 7/8 in.) or smaller

Date from inventory.
Job no. 306.
Many of the drawings contain the notation that the sleeping balcony on the second floor has been omitted.
Scale varies.
This set consists of 10 blueprints with pencil and crayon markings on paper, 6 pencil drawings on paper, 5 ink drawings on cloth with pencil markings and also with ink wash applied to the verso, 5 ink drawings with pencil markings on cloth, 2 blueprints on paper, 2 blueprints with pencil markings on paper, 1 pencil and ink drawing on tracing paper, and 1 blueprint with crayon markings on paper.
