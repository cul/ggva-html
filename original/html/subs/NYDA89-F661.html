Earle C. Anthony House (Los Angeles, Calif.). Alterations and Additions
[Alterations to] residence for Earle C. Athony, esq., Los Angeles, [Calif.] / Greene & Greene, Architects. 1913 40 sheets various media 141.3 x 102.8 cm. (55 5/8 x 50 3/4 in.) or smaller

Scale varies.
Job no. 307.
"The house was moved from its original location at 666 S. Berendo Street, Los Angeles, in 1923 [to] 910 N. Bedford Drive, Beverly Hills" -- Greene & Greene, architecture as a fine art, by Randell L. Makinson, Salt Lake City, Peregrine Smith, 1977. p. 184.
This set consists of 17 blueprints on paper, 12 pencil drawings on tracing paper, 7 pencil drawings with crayon on tracing paper, 2 pencil drawings on paper, 2 pencil and ink drawings on tracing paper, 1 blueprint with pencil and crayon on paper, and 1 pencil and watercolor wash drawing on tracing paper.
Date from inventory.
