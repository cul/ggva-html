George S. Hull House (Pasadena, Calif.)
Residence of George S. Hull, M. D., Pasadena, California. : [and] sun room addition / Greene & Greene, Architects. 1897, 1898 11 drawings various media 62.4 x 61.4 cm. (24 5/8 x 24 1/4 in.) or smaller

Date from inventory. Original work 1897, alterations 1898.
Job no. 21.
Most of the drawings contain signatures -- "M. S. Plant, J. P. Dawson, Geo. S. Hull".
Scale 1/4"=1' or 1/2"=1'.
This set consists of 6 ink drawings with pencil markings on cloth, and 5 ink drawings with pencil markings and also with watercolor wash applied to verso on cloth.
