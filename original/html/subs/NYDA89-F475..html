H.K. Bradley House (Pasadena, Calif.)
Residence for H.K. Bradley, Esq. at Pasadena, Cal. : Part1] / Greene & Greene, Architects. 1909-1910. 131 sheets various media 54 x 76 cm. (21 2/8 x 30 in.) or smaller

Scale varies.
Job no. 254.
Set includes slips of paper used as labels for each different scheme, a slip of paper used aas a ruler, and an illustration of an automobile cut from a magazine. None of these times were assigned an accession no. or filmed.
This set consists of 44 blueprints on paper, 39 pencil drawings on tracing paper, 23 pencil and crayon drawings on tracing paper, 11 blueprints with crayon markings on paper, 11 pencil drawings with ink on tracing paper, 4 pencil drawings on paper, 1 blueprint with pencil markings on paper, and 1 pencil and crayon and ink drawings on tracing paper.
