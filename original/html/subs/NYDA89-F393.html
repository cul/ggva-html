Lucretia R. Garfield House (South Pasadena, Calif.)
Residence for Mrs. Lucretia R. Garfield at South Pasadena, Cal. / Greene & Greene, Architects. 1904 12 drawings various media 56.9 x 63.9 cm. (22 4/8 x 25 2/8 in.) or smaller

Client was the widow of President James Garfield.
Job no. 129.
Scale varies.
This set consists of 7 ink drawings on cloth, 3 ink drawings on cloth with ink wash applied to the verso, and 2 ink and ink wash drawings on tracing paper.
