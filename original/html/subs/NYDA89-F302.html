S.S.Crow House (Pasadena,Calif.). Garage. Addition
Addition to garage for Edward S. Crocker, Esq. at Oak Knoll, Pasadena, California. / [Greene & Greene, Architects] 1911 3 drawings various media 42.7 x 40.5 cm. (16 7/8 x 16 in.) or smaller

Scale 1/4"=1'.
Job no. 271.
"The design for the Crow house is also identified with Edward S. Crocker, who purchased the house in January of 1911 and who has often been identified as the initial client. The records are clear, however, that Henry Greene designed the beautiful bungalow for Dr. Crow ..." -- from Greene & Greene, architecture as a fine art, by Randell l. Makinson, Salt Lake City, 1977, p. 181.
This set consists of 2 pencil drawings on tracing paper and 1 pencil drawing with crayon applied to the verso on tracing paper.
