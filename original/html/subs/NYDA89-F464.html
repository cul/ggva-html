John C. Bentz House (Pasadena, Calif.)
Residence for Mrs. Louise Bentz at Pasadena, Cal. : [with garage and addition] / Greene & Greene, architects. 13 drawings various media 83.8 x 65 cm. (33 x 25 5/8 in.) or smaller

Scale varies.
Job no. 262.
Name of client changed on drawings from John C. Bentz to Mrs. Louise Bentz. Referred to as the "John C. Bentz house, 657 Prospect Square" in Greene & Greene, architecture as a fine art, by Randell L. Makinson, Salt Lake City, Peregrine Smith, 1977.p. 142.
This set consists of 3 ink drawings with pencil and also with ink wash applied to the verso, 3 pencil drawings on tracing paper, 2 ink drawings with pencil on cloth, 2 pencil drawings with ink and crayon on tracing paper, 1 pencil drawing on paper, and 1 ink drawing with ink wash applied to the verso on cloth.
