William B. Tomkins House (Pasadena, Calif.)
House for Wm. B. Tomkins near Pasadena, Cal./ Greene & Greene, Architects. 1898 15 sheets various media 59.4 x 82.8 cm.(23 3/8 x 32 5/8 in.) or smaller

Scale 1/4"=1', 1/2"=1' or unknown.
Foems part of the Greene & Greene collection of architectural drawings;job no.29.
Many of the drawings contain approval signatures -- "Wm.Crowell" and "Wm. B. Tomkins".
This set consists of % ink drawings on cloth with pencil markings and also with watercolor wash applied to the verso, 4 pencil drawings on tracing paper, 4 ink drawings with pencil markings on cloth, and 3 pencil drawings on paper.
