A.M.Drake House (Pasadena, Calif.)
Residence for Mr. A.M. Drake at Oak Knoll, Pasadena, Cal. / Greene & Greene, architects. 1911. 58 sheets various media 59.4 x 95.1 cm. (23 4/8 x 37 4/8 in.) or smaller.+

Scale varies.
Job no. 266.
This set of drawings includes 3 sheets of paper and 2 envelopes which contain notations and correspondence. These were not assigned accession nos., nor were they filmed.
This set consists of 24 pencil drawings on tracing paper, 8 ink drawings on cloth, 6 pencil and ink drawings on tracing paper, 6 blueprints on paper, 6 pencil drawings on paper, 5 blueprints with crayon markings on paper, 3 ink drawings with crayon markings on cloth, 1 blue line print with pencil markings on paper, and 1 ink drawing with pencil markings on tracing paper.
