Margaret B.S. Clapham Spinks House (Pasadena, Calif.)
Residence for Mrs. Margaret B.S. Clapham Spinks at Oak Knoll, [Pasadena], Cal. / Greene & Greene, Arch'ts. 1909. 31 sheets various media 53.6 x 90.3 cm. (21 1/8 x 35 5/8 in.) or smaller

Scale varies.
Job no. 212.
This project is also known as the William Ward Spinks house.
This set consists of 6 pencil drawings on cloth, 5 blueprints with crayon markings on cloth, 5 blueprints with crayon markings on paper, 5 ink drawings with pencil markings on cloth, 4 ink drawings on cloth with pencil markings and also with ink wash applied to the verso, 3 blueprints on paper, 3 blueprints with crayon and pencil markings on cloth, 1 ink and ink wash drawing with pencil markings on tracing paper, 1 ink drawing on cloth with ink wash applied to the verso, 1 blueprint with crayon and pencil markings on paper, 1 ink and ink wash drawing with pencil markings on cloth, 1 blueprint on cloth, and 1 pencil drawing on tracing paper.
