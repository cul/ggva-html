William T. Bolton House, 131 North Euclid Avenue (Pasadena, Calif.)
House for Dr. Wm. T. Bolton, Pasadena, Cal. / Greene & Greene, architects. 1898-1899 12 drawings ink on cloth with pencil markings 38.4 x 41.3 cm. (15 1/8 x 16 2/8 in.) or smaller

Date from inventory.
Job no. 38.
Address from Greene & Greene guide, by Janann Strand, Pasadena, 1974, p. 38.
These drawings are for the first of three houses designed by Greene & Greene for William T. Bolton.
Most drawings include approval signatures -- "M.S. Plant, J.P. Dawson, Dr W. J. Bolton".
Many of the drawings have water damage.
Scale 1/4"=1', 1/2"=1* or 1"=1'.
