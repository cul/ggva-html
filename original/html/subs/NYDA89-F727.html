Carrie Whitworth House (Pasadena, Calif.). Alterations and Additions
[Alterations], additions to residence [and grounds], the Whitworth estate, Lincoln Ave., Pasadena, [Calif.]. / Greene & Greene, Arch'ts. 1919-[19]20. 55 sheets various media 89.7 x 100.5 cm. (35 3/8 x 39 5/8 in.) or smaller

Scale varies.
Job no. 383.
This set includes a typed sheet and carbon titled "Bidders take notice" concerning connection to sewer lines, dated March 4, 1920. These sheets were neither accessioned nor photographed.
This set consists of 33 pencil drawings on tracing paper, 8 blueprints on paper, 5 blueprints with pencil markings on paper, 3 pencil and croyon drawings on tracing paper, 2 pencil and ink drawings on tracing paper, 2 pencil drawings on paper, 1 blueprint with crayon markings on paper, and 1 pencil drawing on tracing paper with watercolor wash markings.
