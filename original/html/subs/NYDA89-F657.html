Earle C. Anthony House (Los Angeles, Calif.)
Residence for Earle C. Anthony, Esq. at Los Angeles, Cal. : / Greene & Greene, Architects. 1909. 31 sheets various media 78.4 x 99.4 cm. (30 7/8 x 39 1/8 in.) or smaller

Many of the drawings are water damaged.
Job no. 238.
"The house was moved from its original location at 666 S. Berendo street, Los Angeles, in 1923 [to] 910 N. Bedford drive, Beverly Hills" -- Greene & Greene architecture as a fine art, by Randell L. Makinson, Salt Lake City, Peregrine Smith, 1977. p. 184.
This set consists of 9 blueprints on paper, 7 ink drawings with ink wash applied to the verso on cloth, 6 ink drawings with pencil on cloth, 4 blueprints with crayon on paper, 3 pencil and ink drawings on tracing paper, and 2 ink drawings on cloth.
