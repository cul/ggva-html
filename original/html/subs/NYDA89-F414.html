Winthrop B. Fay House (Pasadena, Calif.)
Residence for Winthrop B. Fay, Esq. Pasadena, Cal. / Greene & Greene, architects. 31 sheets various media 77.5 x 76.7 cm. (30 1/2 x 30 1/4 in.) or smaller

Scale varies.
Job no. 28.
Some of the drawings contain signatures -- "M.S. Plant, James P. Dawson, W.B. Fay (owner)".
This set consists of 10 ink drawings on cloth with pencil markings and also with watercolor wash applied to the verso, 5 pencil drawings on paper, 3 ink drawings with pencil markings on cloth, 4 ink and watercolor wash drawings on tracing paper, 2 pencil and watercolor wash drawings on paper, 2 pencil drawings on tracing paper, 2 ink and watercolor wash drawings with pencil markings on tracing paper, 2 ink drawings on tracing paper, 2 ink and pencil drawings on paper, 1 ink drawing with pencil markings and also with watercolor wash applied to the verso on tracing paper, and 1 ink and watercolor wash drawing on cloth.
