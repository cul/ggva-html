Residence for Dr. Edw. H. Angle, Pasadena, Cal. : / Greene & Greene, Architects. 1913 21 drawings various media 59 x 57.5 cm. (23 1/4 x 22 5/8 in.) or smaller +

Many of the drawings have water damage.
Job no. 399.
Set includes booklet of "plumbing specifications" which was not assigned an accession no. or filmed.
Scale 1/4"=1' or unknown.
Date taken from inventory.
This set consists of 18 pencil drawings on tracing paper and 3 pencil and crayon drawings on tracing paper.
