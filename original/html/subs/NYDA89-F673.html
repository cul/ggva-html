F.W. Hawks House (Pasadena, Calif.). Unexecuted Design
Residence for F.W. Hawks, Esq. at Pasadena, Calif. / Greene and Greene, Arch'ts. July 12th,1906 8 sheets various media 44.6 x 80.4 cm. (17 5/8 x 31 5/8 in.) or smaller

"Discarding the exciting but more costly first design, the Hawks ... proceeded with a version of the Bentz design ... " -- from Greene & Greene, architecture as a fine art, by Randell L. Makinson, Salt Lake City, 1977, p. 143.
This set consists of 4 blueprints on paper, 2 ink drawings on cloth with ink wash applied to the verso, 1 pencil drawing on paper, and 1 ink drawing on cloth.
