California House
[Design for a California house] / Greene & Greene, Architects. n.d. 25 sheets various media 39 x 56.2 cm. (15 3/8 x 22 1/8 in.) or smaller+

Scale 1/4"=1' and 1/2"=1'.
This set consists of 16 blueprints on paper, 5 pencil drawings on tracing paper, and 4 ink drawings on cloth with ink wash applied to the verso. It also contains three booklets with printed and typescript material entitled "Specifications for a California house".
