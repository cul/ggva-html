Louise T. Halsted House (Pasadena, Calif.). Preliminary Scheme
Residence for Mrs. Louise T. Halsted at Pasadena, Cal. / Greene & Greene, Architects. ca. 1905 6 drawings various media 47.8 x 59.2 cm. (18 7/8 x 23 3/8 in.) or smaller

Date form inventory.
Referred to as the "S. Hazard Halsted house" in Greene & Greene, architecture as a fine art, by Randell Makinson, Salt Lake City, 1977. p. 104.
This set consists of 5 ink drawings on cloth with watercolor wash applied to the verso, and 1 ink drawing on cloth.
