Pasadena Drapery Shop Building (Pasadena, Calif.)
Greene, Henry Mather, 1870-1954.
Brick shop building for Pasadena Drapery Shop, So. Raymond Ave., Pasadena, [Calif.] : / Henry M. Greene, Architect. 1923. 24 sheets various media 61.1 x 69.3 cm. (24 1/8 x 27 1/4 in.) or smaller

Scale varies.
Job no. 454.
Some of the sheets have title information "Brick Shop building for Ernest E. Wennerberg".
This set consists of 15 pencil drawings on tracing paper, 6 blueprints on paper, 2 pencil drawings on paper, 1 blueprint with pencil markings on paper, and 1 blueprint with crayon markings on paper.
