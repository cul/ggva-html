Louise T. Halsted House (Pasadena, Calif.)
Residence for Mrs. Louise T. Halsted at Pasadena, Cal. : [and addition] / Greene & Greene, architects. 13 sheets various media 58.3 x 75.7 cm. (23 x 29 7/8 in.) or smaller

Scale varies.
Job no. 151.
Referred to as the "S. Hazard Halstead house" in Greene & Greene, architecture as a fine art, by Randell Makinson, Salt Lake City, 1977, p. 104.
This set consists of 5 ink drawings on cloth with pencil markings and also with ink wash applied to the verso, 3 ink drawings with pencil markings on cloth, 3 pencil drawings on paper, 1 pencil and ink drawing on tracing paper, and 1 pencil drawing with watercolor wash on tracing paper.
