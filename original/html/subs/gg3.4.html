Photographs by Lewis Josselyn

Biographical/Historical Note

Lewis Josselyn (1883-1964) was an American photographer, active in Carmel, Calif., 1914-1940. His work can be found in the collections of the San Francisco Maritime National Historical Park and the Monterey Peninsula Museum of Art.

Bosley. London: Phaidon, 2000, p.206.
