Date partially from inventory.
Job no. 364.
Many of the drawings in this set are damaged.
Scale varies.
This set consists of 34 pencil drawings on tracing paper, 9 pencil drawings on paper, 6 pencil and ink drawings on tracing paper, 3 pencil drawings on tracing paper with crayon applied to the verso, 1 brownprint on paper, 1 pencil and crayon drawing on tracing paper.
