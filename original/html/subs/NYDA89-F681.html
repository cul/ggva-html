Kinney-Kendall Building (Pasadena, Calif.)
Business block for Joseph N. Kinney at Pasadena, Cal. / Greene & Greene, Architects. 1896 20 drawings various media 66.7 x 98.8 cm. (26 1/4 x 39 in.) or smaller

Many of the drawings contain approval signatures.
Scale 1/4"=1', 1/2"=1' or full size.
This set consists of 11 ink drawings with pencil markings and also with watercolor wash applied to the verso on cloth, and 9 ink drawings with pencil markings on cloth.
