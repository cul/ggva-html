Cordelia A. Culbertson House (Pasadena, Calif.). Alterations and Furniture
Greene, Henry Mather, 1870-1954.
Chair[s], kitchen sink, lantern, chaise longue, [and] front hall, residence for Mrs. F.F. Prentiss, Pasadena, California. / Henry M. Greene, Arch. 1920-1929 13 drawings various media 47.5 x 85.1 cm. (18 3/4 x 33 1/2 in.) or smaller

Scale varies.
Job no. 452.
"Along with their other projects during the 1920's, Charles and Henry were each involved in various additional designs for Mrs. Francis F. Prentiss who had now become as much identified with the Cordelia A. Culbertson house as the Culbertsons themselves" -- Greene & Greene, architecture as a fine art, by Randell L. Makinson, Salt Lake City, Peregrine Smith, 1977, p. 261.
This set consists of 12 pencil drawings on tracing paper and 1 pencil drawing on graph paper.
