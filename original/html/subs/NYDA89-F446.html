Robert R. Blacker House (Pasadena, Calif.). Alterations and Addition
[Addition and alterations] to residence of R. R. Blacker, Esq., at Pasedena, Cal. / Greene & Greene, Architects. 1913. 6 drawings various media 57.5 x 112.6 cm. (22 5/8 x 44 3/8 in.) or smaller

SCale full size or 1" = 1'.
Job no. 315.
This set consists of 4 pencil drawings with crayon on tracing paper, 1 pencil drawin on tracing paper, and 1 pencil drawing on paper.
"Because of the lengthy period of careful maintenance and restoration by the present owners, Mr. and Mrs. Max Hill, the structure has, in recent years, been frequently identified as the Blacker-Hill house" -- Greene & Greene, architecture as a fine art, by Randell L. Makinson, Salt Lake City, Peregrine Smith, 1977, p. 150.
