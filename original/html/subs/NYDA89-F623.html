John H. Poole House (Pasadena, Calif.). Scheme No. 1
Residence for Major J. H. Poole, Pasadena, California. / Greene & Greene, Architects. 1916-1918 90 sheets various media 74.9 x 98.6 cm. (29 1/2 x 38 1/2 in.) or smaller

Most of the drawings are encapsulated in mylar.
Scale varies.
Job no. 369
Set includes blank sheet of tracing paper "kept because of interesting watermark". Sheet was assigned accession no. 1960.001.03084 but was not filmed.
Set includes typewritten letter to Charles Greene with envelope and 10 sheets of paper, most of which contain calculations. None of this material has been assigned an accession no. photographed.
This set consists of 74 drawings on tracing paper, 5 pencil and crayon drawings on tracing paper, 5 pencil drawings on paper, 2 blue line prints with ink wash on paper. 2 blueprints on paper, 1 pencil drawing on tracing paper with chalk applied to the verso, 1 ink drawing on tracing paper, 1 pencil drawing with crayon markings on paper, 1 brown line print with crayon and pencil and ink on paper, 1 ink drawing with pencil markings on tracing paper, 1 pencil drawing with chalk markings on paper, and 1 pencil drawing with ink markings on paper.
