J.H. Jones House (Pasadena, Calif.). Garden and Outbuildings
Garden [and outbuildings for] Mrs. J.H. Jones, 960 N. Los Robles, [Pasadena, Calif.] / Greene & Greene, Architects. 1920 12 drawings various media 50.6 x 64.9 cm. (20 x 25 5/8 in.) or smaller +

Scale varies.
Job no. 404 & 405.
Date from inventory.
This set consists of 2 ink and pencil drawings on tracing paper, 1 pencil drawing on tracing paper with crayon applied to the verso, 6 pencil drawings on tracing paper, 2 pencil and crayon drawings on tracing paper, and 1 pencil drawing on paper. This set also includes 3 sheets of paper with lists of materials, measurements, etc. which were not assigned accession nos. or filmed.
