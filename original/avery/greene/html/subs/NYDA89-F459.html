William T. Bolton House, 370 Del Mar (Pasadena, Calif.). East Porch. Alterations
Greene, Henry Mather, 1870-1954.
Alteration of east porch for Miss Kate Culbertson, 370 Elevado Drive [now Del Mar, Pasadena, Calif.] / Henry M. Greene, Architect. 1926 6 sheets various media 97.7 x 77.3 cm. (38 1/2 x 30 1/2 in.) or smaller

Scale varies.
Job no. 477.
"The Culbertson sisters, after contemplating the construction of a smaller house, purchased the Dr. William T. Bolton house (1906), engaging Henry Greene for alterations shortly thereafter" -- Greene & Greene, architecture as a fine art, by Randell L. Makinson, Salt Lake City, 1977, p. 205.
This set consists of 3 pencil drawings on tracing paper, 2 pencil drawings with crayon on tracing paper, and 1 blueprint on paper.
Date from inventory.
All sheets in this set are encapsulated in mylar.
