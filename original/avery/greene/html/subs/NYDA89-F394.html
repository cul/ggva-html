Lucretia R. Garfield House (South Pasadena, Calif.)
Residence for Mrs. Lucretia R. Garfield at Pasadena, Cal. / Greene & Greene, Architects. 1903 7 drawings various media 61.3 x 61.9 cm. (24 1/8 x 24 3/8 in.) or smaller

Client was the widow of President James Garfield.
Job no. 108.
Date from inventory.
This set consists of 5 ink drawings with pencil markings on cloth, 1 ink drawing with pencil and crayon markings on cloth, and 1 ink drawing with pencil markings on cloth with ink wash applied to the verso.
Scale 1/4"=1', 1/2"=1', or 1 1/2"=1'.
