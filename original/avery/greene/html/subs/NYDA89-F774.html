Mary L. Ranney House (Pasadena, Calif.)
House for Miss Mary L. Ranney at Pasadena. : [440 Arroyo View Drive] / Greene and Greene, Arch'ts ; [Mary L. Ranney] 1907 35 sheets various media 65.6 x 88.5 cm. (25 7/8 x 34 7/8 in.) or smaller

Set includes 3 sheets of paper with measurements for doors, windows, etc. These were not assigned accession nos. or photographed.
Scale varies.
Job no. 203.
"Mary Ranney was a drafts man in the Greene office and was given the opportunity to develop much of the design for her own house" -- Greene & Greene, architecture as a fine art, by Randell L. Makinson, Salt Lake City, Peregrine Smith, 1973. p. 146.
This set consists of 10 blueprints with crayon markings on paper, 9 pencil drawings on paper, 4 ink drawings on cloth with ink wash applied to the verso, 4 pencil and crayon drawings on paper, 3 pencil drawings on tracing paper, 2 ink drawings on cloth with pencil markings and also with ink wash applied to the verso, 2 ink drawings with pencil markings on cloth, 1 blueprint on paper, 1 pencil and watercolor wash drawing on paper, 1 blueprint with crayon and pencil markings on paper, and 1 pencil and ink drawing on paper.
