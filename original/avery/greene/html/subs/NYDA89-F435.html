William T. Bolton House, 370 Del Mar (Pasadena, Calif.)
Residence for Dr. W.T. Bolton at Pasadena, Calif. / Greene & Greene, architects. 1906-1907. 17 sheets various media 67.3 x 102.3 cm. (26 4/8 x 40 2/8 in.) or smaller

Scale varies.
Job no. 180.
This is the third of three houses built for William T. Bolton by Greene & Greene.
This set consists of 4 pencil drawings on tracing paper, 4 ink drawings pencil markings on cloth, 3 ink drawings with pencil markings and also with ink wash applied to the verso on cloth, 2 blueprints with crayon on paper, 2 reversed positive photostats on paper, 1 ink drawing with ink wash applied to the verso on cloth, and 1 pencil drawing on paper.
