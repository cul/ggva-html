California Institute of Technology (Pasadena, Calif.). Dugout
Greene, Henry Mather, 1870-1954.
Plans of the "dug-out" for the California Institute of Technology [at Pasadena, California] / Henry M. Greene, Arch't. 1923 8 drawings various media 41.5 x 72.4 cm. (16 3/8 x 28 1/2 in.) or smaller+

Vracketed title elements supplied by cataloger or taken from inventory.
Job no. 453.
Theis set consists of 5 pencil drawings on tracing paper, 2 pencil drawings with crayon on tracing paper, and 1 pencil drawin with ink on paper. It also contains 2 typescript booklets with 3 pages each, both entitled -- "Painting specification for a one story frame building for the California Institute of Technology at Pasadena, California, Aug. 1923". No accession nos. were assigned to the booklets and they were not filmed. The accession no. 1960.001.00703 has been assigned to a slip of paper included in the set which contains the words in crawyon - "A section through on 1/4 scale". This slip of paper was not filmed.
