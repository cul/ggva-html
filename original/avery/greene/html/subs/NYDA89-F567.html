Coulter Dry Goods Company Store (Los Angeles, Calif.)
Store building for the Coulter Dry Goods Company, Seventh & Olive Streets, Los Angeles, Cal. / W.J.Dodd, Architect ; William Richards, Engineer. 1917 16 sheets various media 91.9 x 122 cm. (36 2/8 x 48 1/8 in.) or smaller

Job no. 233 assigned by W.J. Dodd. Job no. 103 assigned by E.W.Metcalf
Many of the blueprints are damaged.
Scale varies.
The project was later altered by Greene & Greene.
This set consists of 6 blueprints on paper, 5 pencil drawings on paper, 4 blueprints with pencil markings on paper, 3 blueprints with crayon markings on paper, and 1 blueprint with crayon and ink marking on paper.
