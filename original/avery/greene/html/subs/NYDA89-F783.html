Mrs. Francis B. Swan House (Pasadena, Calif.)
Greene, Henry Mather, 1870-1954.
Residence for Mrs. Francis B. Swan, Pasadena, [Calif.] / Henry M. Greene, Architect. 1925. 58 sheets various media 80.1 x 84.3 cm. (31 5/8 x 33 1/4 in.) or smaller

Many of the prints in this set have severe water damage.
Job no. 465.
This set includes 16 small sheets of yellow paper containing "notes". Many of these sheets are blank. None of these sheets were assigned accesssion nos. nor photographed.
Scale varies.
This set consists of 40 pencil drawings on tracing paper, 9 pencil and crayon drawing son tracing paper, 7 blueprints on paper, 4 pencil and ink drawings on tracing paper, 1 pencil drawing on paper, and 1 blueprint with pencil markings on paper.
