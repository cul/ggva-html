L.A. Robinson House (Pasadena, Calif.). Sleeping Porch and Entrance Additions
Sleeping porch addition [and] addition [to entrance] to residence for L.A. Robinson at Pasadena, Cal. / Greene and Greene, Arch'ts. 1916-1917. 8 sheets various media 49.9 x 49.8 cm. (19 5/8 x 19 5/8 in.) or smaller

Scale 1/4"=1' or 3/4"=1'.
This set includes a handwritten note dated Nov. 15, 1917 and a telegram dated Oct. 4, 1917. Neither of these items was assigned an accession no. nor photographed.
This set consists of 4 pencil drawings on tracing paper, 2 pencil and ink drawings on tracing paper, 1 blueprint on paper, and 1 pencil and crayon drawing on tracing paper.
