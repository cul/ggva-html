Datus C. Smith House (Pasadena, Calif.). Alterations and Additions
[Alterations and] addition[s] to residence of Mr. Datus C. Smith, at 487 West California St., Pasadena, Cal. / Greene & Greene, Arch'ts ; Louis Du P. Millar, Architect. 1924 32 sheets various media 50.8 x 65.6 cm. (20 x 25 7/8 in.) or smaller

Scale varies.
Date from inventory.
Job no. 406.
The drawings which have been assigned accession nos. 1960.001.03546-1960.001.03553 are blueprints from the original plans for the house and were executed by Louis Du P. Millar (architect) for Ernest N. Wright (original owner). The crayon markings on these prints may have been made by Greene & Greene.The remaining sheets in this set are alterations and additions by Green & Greene.
This set consists of 24 pencil drawings on tracing paper, 7 blueprints on paper, and 2 blueprints with crayon markings on paper.
Included in this set is a sheet of backing paper for a roll of drawings which was assigned the accession no. 1960.00.03574 but was not photographed.
