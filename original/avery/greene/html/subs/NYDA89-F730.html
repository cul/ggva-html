W. D. Valentine Houses (Altadena, Calif.). Alterations and New Work
Greene, Henry Mather, 1870-1954.
[New] cottage[s and alterations to existing structures] for Mr. W. D.Valentine, Altadena, [Calif.]. / Henry M. Greene, Architect. 1912,1922-1924. 67 sheets various media 57.8 x 70.4 cm. (22 3/4 x 27 5/8 in.) or smaller +

Scale varies.
Job no. 438.
This set includes a sheet of "Pasadena Stationery & Printing Co." note paper with notes dated 7/25/24. There is also a sheet of backing paper for a roll of drawings. Neither of these items was accessioned or photographed.
This set consists of 53 pencil drawings on tracing paper, 7 pencil drawings on paper, 4 pencil and ink drawings on tracing paper, 3 ink drawings with pencil markings on tracing paper, 2 blueprints with crayon markings on paper, 1 blueprint on paper, and 1 blueprint with pencil markings on paper.
