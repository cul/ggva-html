Cordelia A. Culbertson House (Pasadena, Calif.). Alterations to Grounds
[Alterations to grounds], Mrs. Francis F. Prentiss property, Oak Knoll, Pasadena / Greene & Greene, Architects. 1920-1929 11 sheets various media 43.9 x 80.2 cm. (17 3/8 x 31 5/8 in.) or smaller

Scale varies.
Job no. 387.
"Along with their other projects during the 1920's, Charles and Henry were each involved in various additional designs for Mrs. Francis F. Prentiss who had now become as much identified with the Cordelia A. Culbertson house as the Culbertsons themselves" -- Greene & Greene, architecture as a fine art, by Randell L. Makinson, Salt Lake City, Peregrine Smith, 1977, p. 261.
This set consists of 7 pencil drawings on tracing paper, 2 blueprints with pencil markings on paper, 1 pencil drawing on paper, 1 blueprint with crayon and pencil markings on paper, and 1 blueprint on paper.
