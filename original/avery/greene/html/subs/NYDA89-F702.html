Greene, Henry Mather, 1870-1954.

These drwings represent more than one scheme for the alterations.
Job no. 424.
Scale varies.
This set consists of 35 pencil drawings on tracing paper, 10 blueprints on paper, 6 ink drawings on cloth, 2 blueprints with pencil and crayon markings on paper, 2 pencil and ink drawings on tracing paper, 1 blueprint with pencil markings on paper, and 1 pencil drawing on paper.
