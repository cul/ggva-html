John Lambert House (Pasadena, Calif.)
Residence for John Lambert Esq. at Pasadena, California. / Greene & Greene, Arch'ts. 1909, 1910 28 sheets various media 78.9 x 89.9 cm. (31 1/8 x 35 3/8 in.) or smaller

Scale varies.
Job no. 242.
"The scale of this project and the Greenes' criteria for craftsmanship made it too costly and the clients turned to other architects for a more modest design" -- from Greene & Greene, architecture as a fine art, by Randell Makinson, Salt Lake City, 1977. p. 189.
This set consists of 10 blueprints on paper, 8 ink drawings on cloth, 2 blueprints with crayon markings on paper, 2 ink drawings on cloth with ink wash applied to the verso, 1 blueprint with crayon and pencil markings on paper, 1 pencil drawing on paper, 1 ink drawing on cloth with pencil markings and also with ink wash applied to the verso, 1 ink and ink wash drawing on cloth, 1 ink drawing with pencil markings on cloth, 1 ink and crayon drawing on graph paper, and 1 ink and pencil drawing on paper.
