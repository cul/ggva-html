S.S. Crow House (Pasadena, Calif.)
Residence for Dr. S.S. Crow at Oak Knoll, Pasadena, Cal. / Greene & Greene, Arch'ts. 1909-[19]10. 23 sheets various media 57.8 x 104.9 cm. (22 3/4 x 41 3/8 in.) or smaller

Scale varies.
Job no. 245.
"The design for the Crow house is also identified with Edward S. Crocker, who purchased the house in January of 1911 and who has often been identified as the initial client. The records are clear, however, that Henry Greene designed the beautiful bungalow for Dr. Crow..." -- from Greene & Greene, architecture as a fine art, by Randell L. MAkinson, Salt Lake City, 1977, p. 181.
Many of the blueprints are damaged.
This set consists of 5 pencil drawings on tracing paper, 5 pencil drawings with ink on tracing paper, 4 blueprints on cloth, 3 pencil and crayon drawings on tracing paper, 3 blueprints with crayon markings on cloth, 2 ink and pencil drawings on cloth, and 1 blueprint with pencil markings on cloth.
