William T. Bolton House, Corner of Garfield Street and Colorado Avenue (Pasadena, Calif.)
House for Dr. Wm. T. Bolton, Pasadena, Cal. : [and] alteration and additions / Greene & Greene, Architects. 1902, 1904. 6 drawings various media 47.4 x 55.1 cm. (18 6/8 x 21 6/8 in.) or smaller

Part of date from inventory.
Job no. 61.
This is the second of three houses designed for William T. Bolton by Greene & Greene. Original work done in 1902, alteration drawings 1904.
This set consists of 4 ink drawings with pencil markings and also with watercolor wash applied to the verso on cloth, 1 ink and ink wash drawing on tracing paper, and 1 ink drawing on tracing paper.
