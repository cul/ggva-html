Cordelia A. Culbertson House (Pasadena, Calif.). Additions. Lathhouse, Etc.
Greene, Henry Mather, 1870-1954.
Lathhouse, gate, chair[s], planting plan[s for] property of Mrs. F.F. Prentiss, Pasadena, [Calif.] / Henry M. Greene, Architect. 1921 20 sheets various media 105.6 x 90.0 cm. (41 5/8 x 35 3/4 in.) or smaller

Scale varies.
Job no. 420.
"Along with their other projects during the 1920's, Charles and Henry were each involved in various additional designs for Mrs. Francis F. Prentiss who had now become as much identified with the Cordelia A. Culbertson house as the Culbertsons themselves" -- Greene & Greene, architecture as a fine art, by Randell L. Makinson, Salt Lake City, Peregrine Smith, 1977, p. 261.
Set includes 6 sheets of yellow paper with notes on plants and other items for the garden. These sheets were neither assigned accession nos. nor photographed.
This set consists of 12 pencil drawings on tracing paper, 3 ink drawings on cloth, 3 pencil drawings on paper, 2 blueprints on paper, and 1 pencil and crayon drawing with ink markings on tracing paper.
