Louise T. Halsted House (Pasadena, Calif.). Alterations and Additions. 1915, 1922
Halsted alterations, [Pasadena, Calif.]. [Part 1] / Greene & Greene, Architects. 1915, 1922 76 sheets various media 40.6 x 60.8 cm. (16 x 24 in.) or smaller +

Scale varies.
Job no. 346.
Many of the drawings contain the notation -- "strong lines indicate new work, dim lines indicate old work".
Set includes a typescript booklet -- "General specifications, alterations and additions to frame dwelling house for Mr. S. Hazard Halsted at Pasadena, California, June, 1915", 16p. This was not assigned an accession no. or filmed.
This set consists of 30 blueprints on paper, 18 pencil drawings on tracing paper, 7 ink and pencil drawings on tracing paper, 6 pencil and crayon drawings on tracing paper, 6 blueprints with crayon markings on paper, 4 blueprints with pencil markings on paper, 3 ink drawings with pencil markings on tracing paper, 2 pencil drawings on paper, 2 ink drawings on tracing paper, 1 ink and pencil drawing with crayon applied to the verso on tracing paper, and 1 blueprint with crayon and pencil markings on paper.
Set includes a backing paper for a roll of drawings which contains some notes but no drawings. This paper was assigned accession no. 1960.001.01554, but was not filmed.
