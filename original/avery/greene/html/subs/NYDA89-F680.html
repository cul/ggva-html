John Lambert House (Pasadena, Calif.). Garage With Keeper's Quarters
Garage and keeper's house for John Lambert Esq., at Pasadena, Calif. / Greene and Greene, Arch'ts. March, 1910. 18 drawings various media 51.9 x 63 cm.(20 1/2 x 24 7/8 in.) or smaller

Scale varies.
"The scale of this project and the Greenes' criteria for craftsmanship made it too costly and the clients turned to other architects for a more modest design" -- from Greene & Greene, architecture as a fine art, by Randell Makinson, Salt Lake City, 1977. p. 189.
This set consists of 14 pencil drawings on tracing paper, 3 pencil and ink drawings on tracing paper, and 1 pencil and crayon drawing on paper.
