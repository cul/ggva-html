Howard Longley House (South Pasadena, Calif.)
House for Howard Longley, [South Pasadena, Calif.]. / Greene & Greene, Architects. 1897 25 sheets various media 53.8 x 64.4 cm. (21 1/4 x 25 3/8 in.) or smaller

Date from inventory.
Job. no. 23.
Many of the drawings include approval signatures -- "Howard Longley, Ella M. Longley, Arthur Gourlay[?]".
Scale varies.
This set consists of 5 pencil drawings on paper, 4 ink drawings with pencil markings on tracing paper, 4 ink drawings on cloth with watercolor wash applied to the verso, 4 ink and watercolor wash drawings with pencil on tracing paper, 3 ink drawings on cloth, 3 ink drawings on cloth with pencil markings and also with watercolor wash applied to the verso, 1 ink drawing on tracing paper, 1 pencil and watercolor wash drawing on paper, 1 ink and watercolor wash drawing on tracing paper, and 1 ink drawing on tracing paper with watercolor wash applied to the verso.
