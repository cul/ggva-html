Pasadena Ice Company Plant (Pasadena, Calif.)
Building for the Pasadena Ice Co., [Pasadena, Calif.] / John Parkinson, Architect, 702 Laughlin Bldg., Los Angeles, Cal. Between 1894 and 1905 10 sheets various media 63.6 x 93.7 cm. (25 1/8 x 36 7/8 in.) or smaller

Scale varies.
"In 1894 Mr. Parkinson settled in Los Angeles, and established practice in the city ... In 1905 he joined Edwin Bergstrom in partnership." -- Biographical dictionary of American architects (deceased), by Henry F. Withey & Elsie Rathburn Withey, Los Angeles, 1970, P. 456.
This set consists of 5 blueprints on paper, 2 blueprints with pencil markings on paper, 1 blueprint with crayon markings on paper, 1 blueprint with pencil and crayon markings on paper, and 1 pencil drawing on paper.
