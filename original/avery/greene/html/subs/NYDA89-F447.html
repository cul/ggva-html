Robert R. Blacker House ( Pasadena, Calif.). Keeper's House, Pergola Garage With Pump House, and Lathhouse.
Outhouses for residence of R. Blacker Esq. at Oak Knoll, Cal. : keeper's house, pergola, garage [with] pump-house, lathhouse / Greene and Greene, Arch'ts. 30 drawings various media 52.4 x 91.2 cm. (20 5/8 x 36 in.) or smaller

Scale varies.
Job no. 207.
Drawings for the garage include the pumphouse which is inside it.
This set consists of 18 pencil drawings on tracing paper, 6 ink drawings on cloth, 2 ink drawings with pencil markings on cloth, 2 pencil drawings on paper, 1 ink drawing with ink wash applied to the verso on cloth, and 1 ink drawing with crayon on cloth.
"Because of the lengthy period of careful maintenance and restoration by the present owners, Mr. and Mrs. Max Hill, the structure has, in recent years, been frequently identified as the Blacker-Hill house" -- Greene & Greene, architecture as a fine art, by Randell L. Makinson, Salt Lake City, Peregrine Smith, 1977, p. 150.
