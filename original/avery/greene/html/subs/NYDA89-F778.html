House for Mrs. Rose J. Rasey, Pasadena, Cal. / Greene and Greene, Architects. 1902 20 sheets various media 64 x 81.7 cm. (25 1/4 x 32 1/4 in.) or smaller

Scale varies.
Job no. 56.
This set consists of 15 ink drawings with pencil markings on cloth, 5 ink drawings on cloth with pencil markings and also with watercolor wash applied to the verso, 1 ink drawing on cloth, 1 pencil and crayon drawing on paper, and 1 pencil drawing on tracing paper.
