Scale varies.
Job no. 206.
This set consists of "pencil drawings on tracing paper, 3 ink drawings with ink wash applied to the verso on cloth, 3 blueprints with crayon markings on paper, 1 ink drawing with pencil markings and also with ink wash applied to the verso on cloth, and 1 pencil and crayon drawing on tracing paper.
