Date from inventory.
Job no. 154.

Scale varies.
This set consists of 56 blueprints on paper, 4 blueprints with crayon markings on paper, 4 blueprints with pencil and crayon markings on paper, 3 ink drawings on cloth, 3 ink drawings on cloth with pencil markings and also with ink wash applied to the verso, 2 pencil drawings on paper, 2 ink drawings with ink wash applied to the verso on cloth, 1 pencil drawing on cloth, 1 crayon and pencil drawing on paper, and 1 ink drawing on cloth with ink wash applied to both the recto and verso.
