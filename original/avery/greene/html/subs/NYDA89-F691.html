Mary E. Cole House (Pasadena, Calif.)
Residence for Mrs. Mary E. Cole at Pasadena, Cal. / Greene and Greene, Arch'ts. 1906-[19]07. 65 sheets various media 93.2 x 109.1 cm. (36 3/4 x 43 in.) or smaller +

Scale varies.
Job no. 195.
Set includes a list in pencil on paper and a small card used as a label. These items were assigned accession nos. 1960.001.00786-1960.001.0787 but were not filmed.
This set consists of 19 pencil drawings on tracing paper, 11 blueprints with crayon markings on paper, 10 pencil drawings on paper, 7 ink drawings on cloth with ink wash applied to the verso, 6 blueprints on paper, 4 ink drawings with pencil markings and with ink wash applied to the verso on cloth, 3 ink drawings with pencil markings on cloth, 2 pencil and crayon drawings on tracing paper, 2 ink drawings on cloth, 1 pencil drawing with ink markings on tracing paper, 1 reversed positive photostat on tracing paper, 1 blueprint with crayon and pencil markings on paper, and 1 pencil and colored pencil drawing on paper.
