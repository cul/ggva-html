Joseph K. Huston House (Pasadena, Calif.)
Residence for J. K. Huston, Esq. at Pasadena, Cal. / [Greene & Greene] 1911 12 sheets various media 86.8 x 50.3 cm. (34 1/8 x 19 7/8 in.) or smaller

Date from inventory.
Job no. 286.
Many of the drawings use the name "Mrs. Lena W. Huston" instead of "J.K. Huston".
Scale 1/4"=1', 3/4"=1', or unknown.
This set consists of 3 blueprints with crayon markings on paper, 3 ink and pencil drawings on cloth, 2 ink drawings on cloth, 2 blueprints on paper, 2 pencil drawings on paper, and 1 pencil drawing on tracing paper.
