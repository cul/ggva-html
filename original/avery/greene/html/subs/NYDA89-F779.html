Keith Spalding Sespe Ranch (Fillmore, Calif.). Bunk House
Bunk-house for Keith Spalding Esq. at Sespe [Ranch, Fillmore], California : / Greene and Greene, Arch'ts. 1910 24 sheets various media 43.1 x 67.8 cm. (17 x 26 3/4 in.) or smaller

Date from inventory.
Job no. 259.
This set includes a sheet of backing paper for a roll of drawings markied "Set no. 1, office copy" and also a fragment of a sheet of backing paper marked "Set no. 3, showing lights located ... M. Spalding". These sheets were assigned accession nos. 1960.001.03634-1960.001.03635. They were not photographed.
This set consists of 12 blueprints on paper, 4 blueprints with crayon markings on paper, 3 pencil and ink drawings on tracing paper, 3 pencil and crayon drawings on tracing paper, and 2 pencil drawings on tracing paper.
