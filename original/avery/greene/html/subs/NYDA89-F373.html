J. Herbert Hall Apartment House (Pasadena, Calif.). Alterations and Additions.
Alteration and addition to apartment house for Mr. J. Herbert Hall, Pasadena, Cal. / Greene & Greene, Architects. 1916 36 sheets various media 65.8 x 63.3 cm. (25 7/8 x 25 in.) or smaller

Job no. 360.
Scale varies.
This set consists of 12 pencil drawings on tracing paper, 9 pencil and ink drawings on tracing paper, 8 blueprints on paper, 4 blueprints with crayon markings on paper, 3 pencil and crayon drawings on tracing paper, and 1 pencil drawing on paper.
