Greene, Henry Mather, 1870-1954.

Scale varies.
Job no. 456.
Many of the blueprints are signed on thr verso -- "Arthur Savage, Edgar N. Bell".
This set consists of 19 pencil drawings on paper, 7 blueprints on paper, 5 blueprints with pencil markings on paper, 4 pencil markings on paper, and 1 pencil and watercolor wash drawing on tracing paper.
