Robert R. Blacker House (Pasadena, Calif.)
Residence for R. R. Blacker, Esp., at Oak Knoll, Pasadena, Cal. / Greene and Greene, Arch'ts. 1906,1907, [19]09, 1947. 32 sheets various media 95.5 x 110.7 cm. (37 5/8 x 43 5/8 in.) or smaller

Scale varies.
Job no. 207.
"Because of the lengthy period of careful maintenance and restoration by the present owners, Mr. and Mrs. Max Hill, the structure has, in recent years, been frequently identified as the Blacker-Hill house" -- Greene & Greene, architecture as a fine art, by Randell L. Makinson, Salt Lake City, Peregrine Smith, 1977, p. 150.
This set consists of 8 ink drawings on cloth, 7 ink drawings with ink wash applied to the verso on cloth, 4 brown line prints on paper, 3 ink drawings with pencil and also with ink wash applied to the verso, 2 positive reduced photostats on paper, 2 ink drawings with pencil on cloth, 2 pencil drawings with crayon on tracing paper, 1 ink drawing with colored pencil on paper, 1 pencil drawing on tracing paper, 1 brown line print with pencil on paper, and 1 blueprint on paper.
