This set consists of 25 pencil drawings on tracing paper, 17 ink drawings with pencil markings on cloth, 5 ink drawings on cloth, 5 pencil drawings on tracing paper with crayon applied to the verso, 5 reversed sepia process prints [?] on paper, 5 blueprints on paper, 3 blue line prints on paper, 2 ink and pencil drawings on tracing paper, 1 pencil drawing on paper, 1 pencil drawing on tracing paper with crayon drawing on paper, 1 brown line print on paper, and 1 blue line print with pencil markings on paper.
