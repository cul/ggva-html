Photographs by Merge Studios

Biographical/Historical Note

Merge Studios was a commercial establishment located in Los Angeles. In addition to their sixteen photographs of Greene & Greene projects listed in this subseries, Merge Studios produced many of the Leroy Hulbert copy prints described in the subseries, Photographic Copies of Leroy Hulbert photographs.

[S.S. Crow house, Pasadena, Calif.] / photograph[s] by Merge Studios [not before 1909] 11 photographic prints gelatin silver, b&w 20.7 x 25.4 cm. (8 1/4 x 10 in.) or smaller

In 1911 Edward Savage Crocker and his wife purchased Dr. Crow's house. "It was Crocker who was responsible for doubling the size of the property by purchasing land to the west of the residence and enhancing it with extensive gardens and outbuildings." -- Greene & Greene / Edward R. Bosley. London : Phaidon, 2000, p.145.
Bosley dates the house to 1909-10.
2 photos are mounted on board. Board measures 41.5 x 33 cm. (16 3/8 x 13 in.)
Stamp on verso: Photograph by Merge Studios, 2115 W. Pico Blvd., Los Angeles 6, Calif. EX 2458.
