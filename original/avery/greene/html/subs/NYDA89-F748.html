Cordelia A. Culbertson House (Pasadena, Calif.). Wardrobe and Alterations to Grounds
[Alterations to house of] Mrs. [Francis] F. Prentiss, 1188 Hillcrest Ave., Pasadena, [Calif.] / Greene & Greene, Arch'ts. 1920-1929 6 sheets various media 61.1 x 77.9 cm. (24 1/8 x 30 3/4 in.) or smaller

Scale varies.
Job no. 377.
"Along with their other projects during the 1920's, Charles and Henry were each involved in various additional designs for Mrs. Francis F. Prentiss who had now become as much identified with the Cordelia A. Culbertson house as the Culbertsons themselves" -- Greene & Greene, architecture as a fine art, by Randell L. Makinson, Salt Lake City, Peregrine Smith, 1977, p. 261.
This set consists of 4 pencil drawings on tracing paper, 1 pencil and watercolor wash drawing on tracing paper, and 1 blueprint on paper.
