Earle C. Anthony House (Los Angeles, Calif.). Garage. Alterations
Alterations to garage of residence for Earle C. Anthony Esq., at Los Angeles, Cal. / Greene & Greene, Architects. 1909-1917? 6 drawings various media 39.5 x 64.4 cm (15 1/2 x 25 3/8 in) or smaller

One of the drawings has the notation "job no. 238" written in by a different hand. This is the job no. for the original house drawings.It is unclear whether or not this is correct.
House originally designed in 1909 (see aviador record). Inventory states that this set of drawings "precedes 'addition to garage, job #374'" which is dated 1917.
This set consists of 4 pencil drawings on tracing paper, 1 pencil and crayon drawing on tracing paper, and 1 pencil and ink and crayon drawingon tracing paper.
"The house was moved from its original location at 666 S. Berendo Street, Los Angeles, in 1923 [to] 910 N. Bedford Drive, Beverly Hills" -- Greene & Greene, architecture as a fine art, by Randell L. Makinson, Salt Lake City, Peregrine Smith, 1977. p. 184. It is unclear whether the garage was also moved.
Scale 1/4" = 1' , 3/4" = 1' or 1 1/2" = 1'.
